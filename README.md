# 📊 Sales Performance & Operational Risks Dashboard

## The Story Behind the Data
Every business collects mountains of sales and operational data, but turning that raw data into actionable intelligence is where the real value lies. I built this **Sales Performance & Operational Risks Dashboard** to help leadership teams move beyond "what happened" and start asking "why it happened" and "what should we do next?"

This project was born out of a desire to bridge the gap between sales growth and operational efficiency. By visualizing how regional managers perform against their targets and tracking the hidden costs of delivery delays, this dashboard serves as a command center for optimizing business operations.

---

## 🚀 What This Dashboard Solves
In this project, I tackled two critical business pillars:

### 1. Driving Sales Accountability
I designed a **Manager Insights Matrix** to highlight regional performance. It’s not just about total sales; it’s about understanding the context—how each manager is performing across different product categories compared to their set targets. This enables data-driven discussions about incentives and territory management.

### 2. Mitigating Operational Risks
Operational excellence is often hampered by unnoticed inefficiencies. I engineered a solution to monitor:
* **SLA Compliance:** Visualizing which shipping methods are failing to meet delivery promises.
* **Return Volumes:** Identifying which product categories are consistently coming back, helping to pinpoint quality control or customer satisfaction issues.

---

## 🛠️ The Technical Journey
Building this was a rewarding challenge in **Power BI**. The highlight of this project was the backend logic:
* **DAX Mastery:** I wrote complex DAX measures to calculate "SLA Status," dynamically identifying "Delayed" vs. "On Time" deliveries based on specific shipping mode rules.
* **Data Transformation:** I cleaned and modeled raw sales data to create a relational database that powers the interactive visuals.
* **UX/UI Design:** I focused on a "clean-minimalist" aesthetic, ensuring the user isn't overwhelmed by data but can derive insights at a glance.
